Robotics

Robotics is a rapidly growing field that blends mechanical engineering, electronics, and computer science to create machines known as robots. These robots are designed to perform tasks that may be dangerous, repetitive, or difficult for humans. With the help of sensors, actuators, and intelligent programming, robots can sense their environment, make decisions, and interact with the world around them.
